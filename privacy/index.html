Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 September 2025

This policy explains how Illuminated Thinking (“we”, “us”) handles your personal data in connection with the Illuminated Pro Hub. It reflects UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Controller: Illuminated Thinking. Contact: info@illuminatedthinking.co.uk.

What we collect

  • Account data (name, email) from your identity provider via Cloudflare Access.
  • Usage data (pages viewed, basic device info) for security/analytics.
  • Support messages you send us.

How we use your data

  • Authenticate you and operate the Hub.
  • Provide support and service communications.
  • Improve security and performance.

Legal bases

  • Contract — to provide the Hub you access.
  • Legitimate interests — security, performance, analytics proportional to risk.
  • Consent — when we ask for it (e.g., optional cookies or marketing).

Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or anonymise it. Specific retention periods may vary by record type and legal requirements.

Sharing

We use trusted processors to run the Hub (e.g., Cloudflare). We don’t sell your personal data. We only share when necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, or to protect rights.

International transfers

Where data leaves the UK/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses).

Your rights

UK/EEA users can request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection. You can also withdraw consent where relied upon. Contact us using the email above.

Cookies & similar tech

We may use essential cookies for login/session security and limited analytics that respect your privacy. You can control non-essential cookies if/when offered.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. No system is 100% secure.

Questions or complaints

Contact us first. You also have the right to complain to the UK ICO: ico.org.uk.

Changes

We’ll update this page when our practices change. Significant changes will be communicated where appropriate.
